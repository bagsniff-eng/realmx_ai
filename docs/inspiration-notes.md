## Inspiration directions

These three new Realm XAI commercials are based on recurring motion patterns from the uploaded inspiration videos in `remotion/public`:

- `Obsidian`: premium dark crypto aesthetic, cinematic object-led hero, sparse copy, luxury pacing.
- `Velocity`: faster message-led pacing, stacked panels, sequential writing, cleaner product narrative.
- `Atlas`: light editorial architecture, ecosystem-first storytelling, restrained 3D network motion.

Patterns carried over intentionally:

- Large readable messaging with very few words on screen at once.
- Faux-3D depth using perspective, layering, glow, and parallax instead of overloaded effects.
- Brand appears early.
- Product promise is explained through systems, trust, privacy, participation, and ecosystem structure.
- Every cut ends with a direct Realm XAI CTA.

Patterns avoided intentionally:

- Green neon themes.
- Dense fake developer-console visuals that imply API tooling the product does not have.
- Overcrowded UI cards that become unreadable in motion.
