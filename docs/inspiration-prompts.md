## Inspiration Video Prompt Library

These prompts were written from the uploaded inspiration videos in `remotion/public` after extracting frames into `docs/inspiration-analysis`. They are not literal copies of the videos. They are structured motion prompts that describe the visual system, pacing, panel density, camera treatment, typography behavior, logo treatment, and overall feeling of each reference.

### 1. `6C5Ad6-LpAkG4E42.mp4`

**What it is doing**
- Starts extremely minimal on white with oversized circular geometry and tiny editorial text.
- Gradually introduces red-underlined message emphasis.
- Hard contrast shift into black sections with bold brand objects and icon clusters.
- Uses a product-tour rhythm: idea, brand, benefit, product proof, then logo close.
- Motion stays crisp and directional rather than floaty.

**Detailed prompt**
Create a 16:9 product commercial with a sharp editorial-to-brand-cinematic transition. Open on a clean white background with a giant cropped circle or soft geometric outline occupying most of the frame, while very small lowercase text appears in the center-left like a design-system annotation. Let the text progressively extend word by word, then underline key words in a saturated red to create emphasis. After this calm intro, cut aggressively into a black brand world with a thick horizontal motion bar or arrow-like module sliding across screen. Introduce clustered profile icons, small product avatars, and a bold circular logo mark that pulses in the center like a brand medallion. Move into benefit headlines such as “Better e-signature”, “Smarter invoices”, “Faster payments”, each entering as clean bold statements on white again, with large icon illustrations that feel modern and playful. Then bring in a chat or workspace UI panel tilted in perspective, showing multiple user rows, messages, and a focused task flow. Close with a centered glowing logo on a dark background. Keep the ad very modular: white scene, black scene, white scene, product scene, dark logo close. Use punchy, high-contrast transitions, sparse but intentional copy, and a strong brand punctuation system.

### 2. `6f_iDDYUQo8qtxrG.mp4`

**What it is doing**
- Alternates between creamy-white and dark warm gradient scenes.
- Very restrained composition with only one main input or module per beat.
- Typography appears in staggered fragments and then becomes a centered statement.
- Uses a soft premium startup look with warm amber glow accents.
- Feels like an elegant landing page commercial, not a noisy ad.

**Detailed prompt**
Create a premium SaaS launch ad in 16:9 with a cream-and-dark alternating visual system. Start on a nearly empty warm-white canvas with a tiny brand glyph and serif or editorial wordmark at the top. Fade into a dark brown-black gradient with a subtle warm glow in the center, where a single input module appears floating in perspective. Animate text in fragments rather than full sentences, so the viewer feels the phrase assembling over time. Reuse the same core input component through several states: empty, typing, focused, confirmed, and processed. On light scenes, keep the component centered and isolated. On dark scenes, give it more dramatic lighting and shadow so it feels cinematic. Add short premium lines like “Think deeper.” and “Create more.” in elegant centered type, then return to the product module. Motion should be smooth, understated, and highly controlled. Never crowd the frame. The power comes from repetition, subtle state changes, and consistent luxury pacing.
